About Pig Dice

Pig is a simple dice game first described in print by John Scarne in 1945. As with many games of folk origin, Pig is played with many rule variations.


In My Pig Dice, the game is played like this:

  • The game is played between two people. After inputting your names, a page will be diaplayed. The player in Player1 goes first.
  • The player clicks on Roll. The app then gives you a random number on the side of the dice. You can then either choose to hold or Rollagain.
  • Incase you click on hold, the app takes the current value and adds it to your points.
  • However, if you were to click on roll, and your dice ends up at 1, the pints that you had got will be zeroed and till will be the next player's turn
